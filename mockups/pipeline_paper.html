Figure 1. EEG‑LeJEPA pretraining. Raw 10‑s / 19‑channel EEG is decomposed by a learnable filterbank into five physiological bands per patch; per‑band patch embeddings are combined into tokens, a random subset of bands is masked, and a 12‑layer criss‑cross transformer (alternating channel‑ and time‑axis attention) encodes the visible tokens. The primary objective Lcf predicts the spectral power of each masked band (a scalar per patch — not a waveform) against a fixed‑filterbank target (stop‑grad; a learnable target collapses to 0), with an auxiliary weak JEPA latent‑consistency term and a SIGReg anti‑collapse regularizer. For downstream tasks the encoder tokens are mean‑pooled to a 512‑d representation.
